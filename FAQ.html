Frequently Asked Questions

DO YOU TAKE BOOKINGS?

Yes, you can book a table for our special family events. We also save room for walk-ins, so feel free to visit!

I HAVE A DIETARY RESTRICTION AND/OR FOOD ALLERGY…WHAT CAN I EAT ON YOUR MENU?

At Jia qi Shop, we prioritize the safety and satisfaction of our customers, especially kids with dietary restrictions. We provide clear information about allergens in our products. If you have an allergy, please inform our staff, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

IS ANYTHING ON YOUR MENU GLUTEN FREE?

Many items in our shop are gluten-free. Please check with our staff for specific products that meet your dietary needs.

DO YOU HAVE ANY VEGAN DISHES?

Yes! We offer several vegan-friendly options suitable for kids. Just let us know your preferences, and we'll tailor meals to meet your dietary requirements.

DO YOU HAVE A KIDS’ MENU?

Absolutely! Our kids’ menu is filled with fun and healthy options that kids love. Check it out!

ARE YOUR STORES DOG FRIENDLY?

Yes! We love pets. As long as they are on a leash, they are welcome in our stores.
