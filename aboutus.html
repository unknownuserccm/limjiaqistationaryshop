Our Story

Jia qi visited Singapore and was inspired to open this shop, focusing on providing essential stationery and supplies for students.

Over the past year, Jia qi Shop has become a beloved destination, known for its inviting ambiance and diverse offerings. From essential stationery to unique items, we've aimed to provide memorable shopping experiences.

At Jia qi Shop, our mission is simple: to provide the best services and offer the finest products. We aim to redefine your shopping experience with excellence in every item and service, making every visit a memorable one.
